Our Story

At Earth Asia Agro, we believe that food should be fresh, natural, and trustworthy. Established on December 10, 2024, we are an agro-based livestock and cattle farm located in Abdar Bidhai, Kaoraid Union, Sreepur, Gazipur, Bangladesh.

Our farm is dedicated to producing premium quality beef, raw cow milk, dairy products, farm-fresh eggs, and agricultural products. Every product we offer is raised and produced with care, responsibility, and a commitment to quality. We bring wholesome, farm-direct goodness straight to your family's table.

With a focus on ethical practices, natural farming, and hygienic processing, Earth Asia Agro stands for purity, freshness, and trust.

Our Vision & Mission

Building a Sustainable Future

Our Vision

To become a trusted leader in Bangladesh's agricultural sector by providing fresh, high-quality, and responsibly sourced farm products for every household. We envision a future where people choose farm-direct, natural food for a healthier life.

Our Mission

  • • To deliver pure, fresh, and nutritious livestock, meat, milk, eggs, and agricultural products directly to consumers.
  • • To build a brand rooted in trust, transparency, and sustainability.
  • • To practice ethical farming and ensure animal welfare while maintaining the highest hygiene standards.
  • • To empower local communities through employment and economic growth.
  • • To continuously grow and expand, offering more products and services to meet the evolving needs of our customers.
